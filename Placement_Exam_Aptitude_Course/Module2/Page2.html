Classification

Concept Overview

Classification involves grouping items based on common characteristics and identifying the one that does not belong to the group.

1. Types of Classification

A. Word Classification

Identify the word that doesn't fit the category.

Example: Car, Bus, Truck, Ship. (Ship is water transport).

B. Number Classification

Identify the number that breaks the pattern (Prime, Square, Divisibility).

C. Letter Classification

Identify the group of letters with a different pattern.
