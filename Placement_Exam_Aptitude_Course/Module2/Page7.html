Syllogisms

Concept Overview

Syllogisms are a form of logical reasoning where conclusions are drawn from two or more given statements (premises). Venn diagrams are the most effective tool.

1. Basic Statements

  • All A are B.
  • Some A are B.
  • No A is B.
  • Some A are not B.

2. Drawing Venn Diagrams

Draw the minimum overlap diagram that satisfies all statements.

3. Possibility vs. Certainty

A conclusion is valid only if it is 100% true in all possible diagrams.

Detailed Explanation: Either-Or Case

Complementary pairs (Some + No) form "Either-Or" if both are individually invalid.
