Ranking and Order

Concept Overview

Ranking and Order problems involve arranging individuals or items in a sequence based on rank, height, weight, or position.

1. Calculating Total Number

Total N = R1 (Rank from top) + R2 (Rank from bottom) - 1.

Reason: The person is counted twice.

2. Finding Rank from Other End

R2 = (N - R1) + 1.

3. Interchanging Positions

When positions are swapped, use the new rank to find the total number of people or the distance shifted.

Detailed Explanation: Overlapping Cases

To find the number of persons between two ranks:

  • Non-overlapping: Total > R1 + R2. Between = Total - (R1 + R2).
  • Overlapping: Total < R1 + R2. Between = (R1 + R2) - Total - 2.
