Blood Relations

Concept Overview

Blood Relations problems test your ability to understand family connections and deduce relationships between individuals. Drawing a family tree is the most effective way to solve these questions.

1. Basic Relationships

  • Grandfather: Father's Father.
  • Uncle: Father's Brother.
  • Cousin: Uncle's Child.

2. Solving Techniques: The Family Tree

Use symbols for Male (+), Female (-), Spouse (=), Sibling (-).

3. Types of Problems

  • Direct Relations: A is father of B.
  • Pointing to Photograph: "His mother is...".
  • Coded Relations: A+B means A is father of B.
