Seating Arrangement

Concept Overview

Seating Arrangement puzzles test your ability to visualize and organize information. These are among the most common and challenging questions.

1. Linear Arrangement

Facing North (Left is Left). Facing South (Left is Right).

2. Circular Arrangement

Facing Center (Left is Clockwise). Facing Out (Left is Anti-Clockwise).

3. Rectangular Arrangement

People seated at corners or sides.

Detailed Explanation: Complex Puzzles

Combine seating with other variables like profession or blood relation. Use tables or diagrams to map constraints.
