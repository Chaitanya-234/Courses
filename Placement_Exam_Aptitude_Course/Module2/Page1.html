Analogies

Concept Overview

Analogy means "correspondence" or "similarity". In analogy questions, a particular relationship is given between two words, letters, or numbers, and you are required to identify a similar relationship from the given alternatives.

1. Types of Analogies

A. Word Analogies

  • Synonyms: Happy : Joyful
  • Antonyms: Light : Dark
  • Worker and Tool: Carpenter : Saw
  • Cause and Effect: Virus : Disease

B. Number Analogies

Involve mathematical relationships (Squares, Cubes, Primes).

Example: 5 : 25 :: 8 : 64.

C. Letter Analogies

Relationships between letters based on positions.

Example: ABC : ZYX (Opposite pairs).

Detailed Explanation: Approach

  1. Identify the Relationship.
  2. Apply the Rule.
  3. Eliminate Options.
