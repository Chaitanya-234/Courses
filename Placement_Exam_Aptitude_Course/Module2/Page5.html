Direction Sense

Concept Overview

Direction Sense problems test your ability to orient yourself in space and calculate relative positions. These questions involve movement in cardinal (N, S, E, W) directions.

1. Standard Directions

North (Up), South (Down), East (Right), West (Left).

2. Turns

Right Turn = 90° Clockwise. Left Turn = 90° Anti-Clockwise.

3. Distance and Displacement

  • Total Distance: Sum of paths.
  • Displacement: Shortest straight line (Hypotenuse).

Detailed Explanation: Shadow Problems

Morning: Shadow falls West. Evening: Shadow falls East.
