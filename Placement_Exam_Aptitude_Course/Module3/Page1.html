Parts of Speech

Concept Overview

Parts of Speech are the building blocks of the English language. Identifying and using them correctly is the first step towards grammatical accuracy.

1. Noun

Names of people, places, things (Common, Proper, Abstract, Collective).

2. Pronoun

Words used in place of nouns (Personal, Possessive, Relative).

3. Adjective

Describes or modifies a noun.

4. Verb

Expresses an action or state of being (Action, Auxiliary).

5. Adverb

Modifies a verb, adjective, or adverb. Answers How? When? Where?

6. Preposition

Shows relationship between noun and other words (In, On, At).

7. Conjunction

Joins words or clauses (And, But, Because).

8. Interjection

Expresses strong emotion.
