Tenses

Concept Overview

Tenses indicate the time of an action. Mastering the 12 tenses is crucial for constructing correct sentences.

1. Present Tense

  • Simple Present (Habit).
  • Present Continuous (Now).
  • Present Perfect (Completed recently).
  • Present Perfect Continuous (Started in past, continuing).

2. Past Tense

  • Simple Past (Specific past time).
  • Past Continuous (Ongoing in past).
  • Past Perfect (Before another past action).

3. Future Tense

  • Simple Future (Will happen).
  • Future Continuous (Will be happening).
  • Future Perfect (Will have happened).
