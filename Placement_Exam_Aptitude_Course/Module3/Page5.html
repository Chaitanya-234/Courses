Idioms and Phrases

Concept Overview

Idioms are phrases where the meaning cannot be deduced from the literal definitions of the individual words.

1. Business Idioms

Ball rolling, Think outside box, Loop, Ropes.

2. Animal Idioms

Cat out of bag, Kill two birds, Elephant in room.

3. Phrasal Verbs

Give up, Look forward to, Put off, Call off.
