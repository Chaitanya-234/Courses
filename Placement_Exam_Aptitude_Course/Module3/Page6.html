Reading Comprehension

Concept Overview

Reading Comprehension tests your ability to read text, process it, and understand its meaning. It involves identifying the main idea, details, and tone.

1. Types of Questions

Main Idea, Direct Detail, Inference, Tone, Vocabulary.

2. Strategies

  • Skimming: Get the gist.
  • Scanning: Find keywords.
  • Elimination: Remove extreme options.

3. Inference vs Assumption

Inference is based on evidence in text. Assumption is a logical gap.

Detailed Explanation: Tone Analysis

Identify if the author is Narrative, Descriptive, Argumentative, or Analytical.
