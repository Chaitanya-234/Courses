Subject-Verb Agreement

Concept Overview

Subject-Verb Agreement states that the verb must agree with its subject in number and person.

1. Basic Rules

Singular Subject -> Singular Verb. Plural Subject -> Plural Verb.

2. Compound Subjects

Joined by 'and' -> Plural. Joined by 'or' -> Agrees with closer subject.

3. Indefinite Pronouns

Each, Every, One, Anyone -> Always Singular.

4. Collective Nouns

Singular as a unit, Plural as individuals.
