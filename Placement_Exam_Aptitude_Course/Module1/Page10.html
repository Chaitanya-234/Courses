Geometry and Mensuration

Concept Overview

Geometry deals with the properties of space, shapes, and figures. Mensuration focuses on the calculation of lengths, areas, and volumes.

1. Lines and Angles

  • Parallel Lines: Alternate interior angles are equal.
  • Perpendicular Lines: Intersect at 90°.

2. Triangles

  • Equilateral: Area = (√3/4)a².
  • Right-Angled: Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c².

3. Circles

  • Circumference: 2πr
  • Area: πr²

4. Mensuration (3D Solids)

Volume and Surface Area formulas for Cube, Cuboid, Cylinder, Cone, Sphere.

Distance Formula, Slope, Equation of Line.
