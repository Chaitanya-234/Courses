AI Accessibility with Amazon Q Apps

Welcome to AI Accessibility with Amazon Q Apps. In this course, you will learn how to leverage the power of Generative AI to create inclusive, accessible, and empowering tools for everyone in your organization.

Course Overview

As Artificial Intelligence becomes more integrated into our professional lives, it is crucial that these tools are designed with accessibility at their core. This course provides a comprehensive exploration of AI accessibility principles and demonstrates how the no-code environment of Amazon Q Apps can be used to build solutions that meet the needs of a diverse workforce.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the fundamental principles of AI accessibility and inclusive design.
  • Learn how to apply WCAG standards to Generative AI interfaces and outputs.
  • Explore the role of Amazon Q Apps in democratizing the creation of assistive tools.
  • Master prompt engineering techniques for generating accessible AI content.
  • Implement governance and ethical frameworks for managing accessible AI at scale.

Expected Learner Outcomes

By the completion of this course, you will have the knowledge and skills necessary to build, test, and deploy AI applications that are usable by individuals with varying cognitive, physical, and sensory abilities. You will be prepared to serve as an accessibility champion within your organization's AI initiatives.

Target Audience

This course is designed for:

  • Business Users: Who want to build custom tools without writing code.
  • AI Practitioners: Who need to understand how to make their models and applications more inclusive.
  • Compliance & Diversity Officers: Who want to ensure AI deployments meet accessibility standards.
  • HR Professionals: Looking to use AI to improve employee onboarding and support.

Prerequisites

  • Technical: No coding experience is required. Familiarity with basic web navigation is expected.
  • Conceptual: A basic understanding of what Generative AI is and how it is used in a business context.
  • Tools: Access to the Amazon Q Business console (optional but recommended for practical application).

Course Structure

The course is divided into three core modules, each followed by a knowledge check. A final comprehensive assessment is required to complete the program.

  • Module 1: Principles of AI Accessibility
  • Module 2: Building Accessible AI Solutions with Amazon Q Apps
  • Module 3: Governance, Ethics, and the Future of AI Accessibility
