What Is Amazon Q Business?

Overview

Amazon Q Business is a fully managed generative AI assistant for enterprises. It connects to your company's data sources and provides employees with accurate, cited answers based on your internal knowledge.

Key Features

  • 40+ Data Connectors: S3, SharePoint, Confluence, Salesforce, Slack, Gmail, Jira, and more
  • Access Controls: Respects existing document-level permissions from source systems
  • Citation Support: Every answer includes source document references
  • Custom Plugins: Extend Q with custom actions (create tickets, update CRM, etc.)
  • Admin Controls: Topic blocking, response guardrails, usage analytics
