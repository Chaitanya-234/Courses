Advanced PartyRock and Use Cases

Chaining Widgets

Build complex apps by connecting widgets in a pipeline:

  1. User Input: "Enter a business idea"
  2. Text Gen 1: "Create a company name for: @BusinessIdea" (references input)
  3. Text Gen 2: "Write an elevator pitch for @CompanyName that does @BusinessIdea"
  4. Image Gen: "Create a logo for @CompanyName - @ElevatorPitch"
  5. Text Gen 3: "Create a one-page business plan for @CompanyName"

Popular PartyRock App Categories

  • Education: Quiz generators, study helpers, concept explainers
  • Creative: Story writers, poem generators, D&D character creators
  • Productivity: Email drafters, meeting summarizers, task prioritizers
  • Development: Code explainers, regex builders, SQL generators
  • Marketing: Ad copy generators, social media post creators

From PartyRock to Production

PartyRock is for prototyping. To move to production:
